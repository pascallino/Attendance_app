User management and Data security

Create users and assign roles effortlessly, empowering you to manage access levels and permissions efficiently within your organization.

Our app ensures the protection of your database and routes, employing robust security measures to safeguard sensitive information and prevent unauthorized access.

Attendance management Module

Clock In and Clock Out: This feature enables staff to log their start (clock in) and end (clock out) times for their work shifts. Approve/Revoke Attendance: Administrators can revoke or correct attendance records if errors, ensuring the accuracy of attendance data. Admin staff can also approve attendance Reports: Detailed reports on staff attendance, including total hours worked and attendance trends, are generated through this feature.

Seamless Communication

Enable efficient communication via emails throughout the attendance process. Keep administrators informed with automated notifications when staff clock out, ensuring timely updates and enabling effective oversight of staff attendance. This feature enhances the overall management experience by keeping key stakeholders engaged and aware of staff activities in real time.
